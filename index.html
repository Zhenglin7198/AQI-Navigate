About AQI Navigate

Designed for pedestrians and cyclists, AQI Navigate helps you find healthier, safer routes for your journeys.

  • Uses Apple's official MapKit interface for map data and traffic information
  • Color-coded air quality indicators (green = good, yellow = moderate, red = poor)
  • Icons for problem areas (construction, dust, odor)
  • Complete privacy with anonymous data sharing
